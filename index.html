Common Questions

FAQ

Can't find your answer? Reach out on WhatsApp — we reply fast.

Ask on WhatsApp
How long does a project take?
Most designs (logo, business card, CV) are delivered within 24–48 hours. Larger projects like websites may take 3–7 days depending on complexity and content provided.
What file formats do I receive?
You'll receive high-resolution PNG, JPG, and print-ready PDF files. For logos we also provide vector formats (AI, SVG) so you can scale to any size without quality loss.
How many revisions are included?
We offer unlimited revisions until you're fully satisfied. We believe your final design should match your vision exactly — we won't stop until it does.
How do I place an order?
Simply message us on WhatsApp or fill out the contact form on this page. Share your requirements, and we'll get back to you with a quote and timeline within a few hours.
What information do you need to start?
For logos: business name, industry, and colour preferences. For CVs: your content/information. For websites: your content and any reference sites you like. The more detail you provide, the better the result!
Do you work with clients outside Sri Lanka?
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. All communication and file delivery is handled digitally, so location is never a barrier.
What are your payment options?
We accept bank transfers and online payments. Payment terms are discussed before work begins — typically 50% upfront for larger projects.
